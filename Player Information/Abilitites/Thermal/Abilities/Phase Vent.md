Base (Phase Vent):
- The player vents a burst of heat behind them to execute a high-speed directional dash, leaving behind a trail of superheated air that inflicts [[Entropy]] on crossing enemies.
- +10 Energy

Ultimate (Thermal Jetstream):
- Active when meter is > 80
- The player gains infinite dash charges for 6 seconds. Dashing directly through an enemy triggers an explosion that instantly grants the player a stack of [[Conduct]] while blinding the target with steam.
- Drains -10 energy per dash until 0 energy or ultimate ends.
