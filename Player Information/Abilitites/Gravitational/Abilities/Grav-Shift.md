Base (Grav-Shift):
- Changes the player's gravity, allowing them to rapidly float upward or safely glide downward, gaining temporary [[Orbit]].
- -10 Energy

Ultimate (Tidal Disruption):
- Active when meter is between 20 and 40
- The player targets a location and switches gravity globally for enemies in that area. Foes are thrown sideways or into walls, taking high impact damage and granting the player [[Orbit]] that begins at 50% for 8 seconds.
- Forces meter to 50.
