Base (Radiator Cell):
- Throws a thermodynamic device that adheres to surfaces, rapidly draining local energy to create a heat-sink dome. Enemies inside are afflicted with [[Entropy]], While allies are given [[Tempered]].
- +20 Energy

Ultimate (Convective Vortex):
- Active when meter is between 45 and 55
- The cell implodes, violently sucking all enemies toward it before unleashing a towering fire tornado. It completely strips enemy armor, spreading [[Excitate]] to everything caught in the radius. 
- Locks meter at 50 for 10 seconds.
