Base (Snap Field):
- Throw a device that deploys a high-tension grid across a lane. Enemies crossing it snap the lines, taking damage and getting [[Strain]]ed.
- -20 Energy

Ultimate (Snapback):
- Active when meter is at exactly 0.
- Throw a device that deploys a single high-tension line. Enemies crossing it cause the line to seek out other nearby enemies, grabbing all enemies within 10 meters, snapping them inward and inflicting [[Strain]].
- Pushes meter to 30, forces to 100 on contact.
