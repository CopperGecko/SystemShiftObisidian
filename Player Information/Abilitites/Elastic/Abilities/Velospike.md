Base (Velospike):
- Thrust a high-velocity spike that nails an enemy to the floor or wall, inflicting [[Tether]].
- -15 Energy

Ultimate (Giant Velospike)
- Active when meter is < 20.
- Thrust a heavy spike. Upon impact, it latches onto all nearby enemies, and violently launches them upward into the ceiling or walls at terminal velocity, inflicting [[Strain]].
- Forces meter to 80 for 5 seconds or until all enemies affected die.
