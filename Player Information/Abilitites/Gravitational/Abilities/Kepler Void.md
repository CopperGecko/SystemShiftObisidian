Base (Kepler Void):
- Throws a singularity core that creates a vortex, triggering [[Singularity]] to pull in minor mobs and collect loose drops to a single point. Grants [[Accretion]]
- -20 Energy

Ultimate (Supermassive Well):
- Active when meter is > 70
- The vortex expands to triple its normal size and lasts twice as long. It completely locks down bosses, prevents elite enemies from using abilities, and constantly applies [[Collapse]] to everything trapped inside. Instantly drops energy meter to 0.
