Base (Event Horizon):
- A heavy downward overhead strike that slams the ground, creating a micro-void that inflicts [[Collapse]] on all enemies within a 3-meter radius. 
- -15 Energy

Ultimate (The Big Crunch):
- Active when meter is at exactly 0
- The player leaps high into the air and crashes down like a meteor. The impact creates a massive cosmic field where all enemies are lifted into zero-G for 3 seconds, then violently slammed back down dealing damage and inflicting [[Collapse]].
- Resets meter to 50.
